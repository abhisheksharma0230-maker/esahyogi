ℹ️ About Us

Citizen-First Platform

What is 𝒆 𝑺𝒂𝒉𝒚𝒐𝒈𝒊?

𝒆 𝑺𝒂𝒉𝒚𝒐𝒈𝒊 is the official citizen complaint management system of the Government of India. It is part of the Digital India initiative and helps citizens reach government departments efficiently. Our platform leverages modern technology to ensure transparent, accountable, and swift resolution of civic issues.

24/7 Citizen complaint support available across the platform.
AI Routing Smart department suggestion for faster complaint handling.
Real-Time Track progress, updates, and resolution status instantly.
Secure Citizen data, complaint records, and media stay protected.
Purpose & Mission

Our Mission

  • 🎯Transparent and accountable governance system for every citizen complaint.
  • 📢Amplifying citizens' voices and connecting them directly with the right department.
  • ⚡Fast and effective digital workflows that reduce delays in action.
  • 💻Inclusive digital empowerment so services feel simple and accessible for all.
Technology Stack

Technical Features

  • 🔒Secure data encryption and privacy protection across citizen and admin flows.
  • 🌐Multilingual support with Hindi and English switching across major pages.
  • 📱Mobile-friendly responsive design for phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • 🤖AI-powered smart complaint routing to guide issues to the correct department.
  • 🎤Voice-to-text support for easier complaint submission and accessibility.
  • 📊Real-time analytics, tracking, and reporting for transparent monitoring.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security is our top priority

Information We Collect

  • • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address for complaint registration
  • • Complaint Data: Details of your grievances, supporting documents, photos
  • • Technical Data: IP address, browser information, usage patterns (anonymized)
  • • Location Data: Area/PIN code for complaint routing (optional)

How We Use Your Data

  • • Complaint Processing: Route complaints to appropriate government departments
  • • Communication: Send status updates via SMS/email
  • • Service Improvement: Analyze data to enhance system performance
  • • Legal Compliance: Maintain records as per government regulations

Data Security Measures

  • • Encryption: All data transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • • Access Control: Restricted access to authorized government personnel only
  • • Audit Trails: Complete logging of all data access and modifications
  • • Regular Backups: Secure data backup and disaster recovery systems

Your Rights

  • • Access: View all personal data we have about you
  • • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • • Deletion: Request data deletion (subject to legal requirements)
  • • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
